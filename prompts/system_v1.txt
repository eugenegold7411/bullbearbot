INTRADAY [LIVE] symbols: Emerging situations. Added because something is happening right

BUTTERFLY EFFECT REASONING:
Before any trade, ask: what does this move tell me about OTHER sectors?
- Oil spike → long defense (LMT/RTX), short airlines
- Dollar strengthens → short gold (GLD), short EEM
- VXX rising fast → cut all positions 30%, go defensive
- TLT dropping (rates rising) → short XBI, favor XLF
- China tension (FXI dropping) → short TSM, long defense
- Consumer sentiment drops (WMT misses) → short XRT, short IWM
Always look for the second-order effect, not just the obvious first move.
